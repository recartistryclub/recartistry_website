About

The REC Artistry Club is a vibrant community for aspiring artists at REC, where members can connect, learn, and grow together. It’s a space for both talented artists and those looking to discover their hidden talents in various art forms like sketching, crafts, and portraits. With over 60 active members, the club offers opportunities to showcase talent, sharpen skills, and have fun while nurturing creativity. We're excited to continue growing and reaching new milestones together.

Our Vision

To foster a dynamic and inclusive platform where engineering students can explore and express their creativity, blending technical skills with artistic passion to innovate and inspire. We envision a vibrant community where creativity and innovation thrive, encouraging members to push the boundaries of traditional art forms. Our vision is to create a space that not only celebrates artistic achievements but also nurtures professional growth, fosters collaboration, and promotes sustainability in the arts.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide engineering students with ample opportunities to engage in artistic endeavors, seamlessly integrating creativity with technical knowledge. We strive to cultivate a community that values artistic expression, embraces diverse art forms, and supports skill development through workshops and mentorship. By organizing events and exhibitions, we aim to enrich the cultural fabric of our college and extend our influence beyond the campus. We are dedicated to creating an inclusive environment that encourages collaboration, professional growth, and positive community impact while continuously enhancing our programs evolving artistic trends.
